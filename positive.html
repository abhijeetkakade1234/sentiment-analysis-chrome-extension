Ocean Cleanup Device Successfully Removes 500,000 Kg of Plastic

Published on June 9, 2025

In a major milestone for ocean conservation, The Ocean Cleanup project has announced that its latest system has successfully removed over 500,000 kilograms of plastic from the Great Pacific Garbage Patch. The team attributes the success to its improved System 03, which has been operating continuously for several months.
